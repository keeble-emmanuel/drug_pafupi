    ABOUT DRUG PAFUPI

    MISSION STATEMENT

    To enhance public health in Malawi by connecting people to the medications they need, when and where they need them, through an accessible and efficient digital platform.

    WHY DRUG-PAFUPI

    A relible way to know which pharmacies have a specific drug in stock, reducing wasted trips.

    Finding pharmacies nearby that stock the required medication.

    Reducing the time and transport costs associated with searching for drugs.

    Timely access to medication, especially for urgent needs or chronic conditions.
